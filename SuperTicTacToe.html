Super Tic Tac Toe

Rules

  1. Rules are very similar to a standard game of Tic Tac Toe with the exceptions listed below
  2. The game is played on a 3 x 3 grid of 3 x 3 Tic Tac Toe boards(9 x 9 in total)
  3. The first player gets to place their mark anywhere on the board.
  4. The following player must play in the smaller game aligned with the space the previous player marked
    • Ex: Player 1 marks the top left square of the center board so Player 2 must place their mark in the top left GAME
  5. Repeat step 4 until one of the smaller games has a winner
  6. The winner of the smaller game claims that section of the larger board
    • If a player is forced to play on a board that has already been claimed they can place a mark anywhere like in step 3
  7. The game is over when a player has three marks in a row in the larger game board
